SUVIDHA CIVIC FAQ — Vijayawada Municipal Corporation

=== WATER CONNECTION ===
Q: How do I apply for a new water connection?
A: To apply for a new water connection in Vijayawada, visit your nearest ward office with the following documents: Aadhaar card, property tax receipt, passport size photograph, and a filled application form. The processing fee is Rs 500 for domestic connections and Rs 2000 for commercial connections. Processing takes 7-10 working days.

Q: What is the water bill due date?
A: Water bills are generated on the 1st of every month and must be paid by the 15th of the same month. Late payment attracts a penalty of 2% per month on the outstanding amount.

Q: How do I report a water supply complaint?
A: You can report water supply issues through the SUVIDHA portal under the Complaints section. Select "Water Supply" as the category. You can also call the helpline at 0866-2570000. Emergency water supply issues are addressed within 24 hours.

Q: How is the water bill calculated?
A: Water bills are calculated based on units consumed per month. 0-10 units: Rs 5 per unit. 11-20 units: Rs 8 per unit. Above 20 units: Rs 12 per unit. A minimum charge of Rs 50 applies even if consumption is zero.

=== ELECTRICITY ===
Q: How do I apply for a new electricity connection?
A: Submit an application at the APEPDCL office with your Aadhaar card, property documents, and passport photo. For domestic connections the fee is Rs 1500. Processing takes 5-7 working days after document verification.

Q: How do I pay my electricity bill?
A: You can pay electricity bills through the SUVIDHA portal under Bill Management. We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, and net banking through Razorpay payment gateway.

Q: What do I do if there is a power outage?
A: Report power outages through the SUVIDHA Complaints section selecting "Electricity" category. You can also call APEPDCL helpline 1912. Planned outages are announced 24 hours in advance.

=== PROPERTY TAX ===
Q: How is property tax calculated in Vijayawada?
A: Property tax in Vijayawada is calculated based on Annual Rental Value of the property. Residential properties: 17-30% of ARV. Commercial properties: 25-40% of ARV.

Q: When is property tax due?
A: Property tax is payable in two installments. First installment: April to September. Second installment: October to March. Early payment before April 30 attracts a 5% rebate.

Q: How do I pay property tax?
A: Property tax can be paid through the SUVIDHA portal under Bill Management. You can also pay at the Municipal Corporation office or designated banks.

=== COMPLAINTS ===
Q: How do I file a complaint?
A: Log in to SUVIDHA portal and go to the Complaints section. Click File Complaint. Fill in the title, description, category, and location. You can attach up to 3 photos as evidence. You will receive a unique complaint number for tracking.

Q: How long does it take to resolve a complaint?
A: Resolution time depends on complaint category. Roads and potholes: 7-15 working days. Streetlight issues: 2-3 working days. Water supply: 1-3 working days. Drainage issues: 3-7 working days.

Q: How do I track my complaint?
A: Go to Complaints section in SUVIDHA. Click on any complaint to see the status timeline. Statuses are: Pending, In Progress, Resolved, Closed.

Q: What complaint categories are available?
A: SUVIDHA supports these complaint categories: Roads and Potholes, Water Supply, Electricity, Sanitation, Streetlights, Drainage, Parks and Gardens, Noise Pollution, and Other.

=== BILLS AND PAYMENTS ===
Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: SUVIDHA accepts UPI, credit cards, debit cards, and net banking through Razorpay payment gateway. All transactions are secured and encrypted.

Q: What happens if my bill is overdue?
A: Bills that cross their due date are automatically marked as Overdue in the system. Pay overdue bills immediately to avoid service disruption.

=== GENERAL ===
Q: How do I update my profile?
A: Go to My Profile section in the SUVIDHA dashboard. You can update your phone number, address, and ward details.

Q: What is Aadhaar verification?
A: Aadhaar verification confirms your identity as a genuine citizen. Verified citizens get priority processing for service requests and complaints.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach SUVIDHA support through the AI Assistant for instant answers. For urgent issues call Vijayawada Municipal Corporation helpline: 0866-2570000. Office hours are Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

Q: What is SUVIDHA?
A: SUVIDHA stands for Smart Urban Virtual Interactive Digital Helpdesk Assistant. It is the official digital portal of Vijayawada Municipal Corporation for citizens to access all municipal services online.

=== SERVICE REQUESTS ===
Q: How do I apply for a gas connection?
A: Apply for a gas connection through the Service Requests section in SUVIDHA. You need Aadhaar, address proof, and passport photo. The connection is processed within 15 working days.

Q: What services can I request through SUVIDHA?
A: SUVIDHA supports service requests for Water Connection, Electricity Connection, and Gas Connection.
